Profiles

Beans

Hi, I'm Beans. I am the leader of the Guinea Girls and it is my job to make sure my sisters are happy. I am also the most friendly guinea pig and most importantly, lead squeaker. Here is some information about me you may find interesting:

Full Name: Beans

Colour: White, black and gold

Age: 7 and a half months

Likes: Being hand fed romaine lettuce and other veg, our ramp, being at home

Strengths: Squeaking for veg, resting, climbing, exploring

Pingu

Hi, I'm Pingu. I am a very laid back guinea pig and am the shyest member of the Guinea Girls. I get ever so nervous but I am lovely once you get to know me. Feel free to check out some more information below:

Full Name: Pingu

Colour: Black and gold

Age: 7 and a half months

Likes: Romaine lettuce, our wooden cabin, nibbling on our plastic tunnel, doing laps of the cage with Dooda

Strengths: Pretending to be a statue, hiding

Dooda

Hi, I'm Dooda. I am the mischievous one and it is my job to cause lots of trouble. I am also the most daring and fastest Guinea Girl, although I make my Mum nervous when I try and jump off high things!

Full Name: Dooda

Colour: White and grey

Age: 7 and a half months

Likes: Romaine lettuce, using the ramp, chewing on anything and everything

Strengths: Distress squeak, speed, jumping from heights, running away, running into furniture
